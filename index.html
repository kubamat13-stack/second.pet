About project

Second Pet is an independent design project created in Poland, born from everyday life with a dog and a love for thoughtful, minimalist objects.

The idea was simple: to create a small, friendly object that doesn’t shout for attention, but quietly rewards it. Something that makes you smile not once, but again and again — every minute.

Our watches are not about specifications, status, or trends. They are about a moment. A tiny interaction between time and illustration. A playful detail that reveals itself only if you stop for a second and actually look.

The inspiration comes directly from life with a dog — waiting, playing, repetition, and small daily rituals that fill ordinary days with warmth.

This is our very first production run. A careful, limited beginning of what we hope will become a growing collection of thoughtful designs. By supporting the project now, you support an independent creator building something slowly, without shortcuts or mass production.

Second Pet is not a corporate brand. It’s a personal project, developed intentionally, with attention to detail and respect for the user.

If you notice small things, enjoy quiet jokes, love dogs, or believe that everyday objects can be kinder and more human — you are in the right place.
