The Watch

A minimalist watch built around one small idea.

The second hand ends with a tennis ball. Once every minute, it reaches the dog’s nose. A quiet detail you notice only if you look closely.

The moving ball is not a printed illusion. It is a custom produced second hand created specifically for this design. It required its own development and tooling, made only for this project.

About the Project

Second Pet is an independent design project based in Poland.

The focus is not on luxury positioning or technical specifications. It is about thoughtful design and small repeatable moments that add character to an everyday object.

Each watch from this batch is physically produced, inspected before shipping, and sent directly from Poland to its new owner.
