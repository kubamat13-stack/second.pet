The Watch

In most watches, the second hand is just a ticking line. At Second Pet, it's the heart of the design.

The concept is built around a custom-made second hand with a small ball at the end. Once every minute, as the hand sweeps the dial, the ball aligns perfectly with the dog's mouth. Whether it's a Dachshund or a Corgi, the dog finally "catches" the ball.

First Batch (Dachshund & Corgi)

Independent production run. Minimalist brushed dial. Soft silicone strap. Shipped from Poland.

The Story

Second Pet is an independent design project created in Poland, born from a love for thoughtful, minimalist objects and a sense of humor.

The idea is rooted in "quiet joy"—those small, private moments that don’t shout for attention but reward it. It’s that small smile you get when you notice your pet catching its ball on your wrist during a busy day.

This isn’t a corporate product. It’s an experiment in design and novelty, developed slowly and intentionally. From the custom-weighted second hand to the clean illustration, every detail is made for people who notice small things.

We are starting with this limited production run to see if others find this one-minute ritual as satisfying as we do. No mass production, no shortcuts—just an independent project sent directly from our home to yours.

Independent project based in Poland. Shipping worldwide.
