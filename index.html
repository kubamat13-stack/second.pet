About project

Second Pet is an independent design project created in Poland, born from everyday life with a dog and a love for thoughtful, minimalist objects.

The idea was simple: to create a small, friendly, and genuinely joyful product that doesn’t shout for attention, but quietly rewards it. A design that makes you smile not once, but again and again, every minute.

Our watches are not about specifications, status, or trends. They are about a moment. A tiny interaction between time and illustration. A playful detail that reveals itself only if you stop for a second and actually look.

The inspiration comes directly from life with a dog — waiting, playing, repetition, and those small rituals that fill ordinary days with warmth. That’s why Second Pet is made for dog lovers, but also for people who appreciate good design, simplicity, and objects with a sense of humor.

This is our very first production run. A small, carefully made beginning of what we hope will become a growing collection of thoughtful designs. By supporting the project at this stage, you are supporting an independent creator, experimenting, learning, and building something step by step without mass production, shortcuts, or empty promises.

Second Pet is not a brand created by a corporation. It’s a personal project, developed slowly and intentionally, with attention to detail and respect for the user. Each design starts with an idea, not a market spreadsheet.

If you are someone who notices small things, enjoys quiet jokes, loves dogs, or simply believes that everyday objects can be kinder and more human, then you are in the right place.
