The Story

Second Pet is an independent project from Poznań, Poland. It was born from a simple observation: most watches treat the second hand as an afterthought. I wanted to turn that around.

The idea is rooted in "quiet joy"—those small, private moments that don’t shout for attention but reward it. Inspired by my Corgi and those repetitive rituals of play, I designed a watch that creates a tiny interaction between time and illustration.

This is not a corporate product. It’s a solo project, built step by step, focused on design and novelty rather than status. I’m starting small with this first production run to see if others find this one-minute ritual as satisfying as I do.

Every watch is shipped personally from my home in Poznań. No shortcuts, just a genuine attempt to make everyday objects a bit more human and a lot more fun.

Based in Poznań, Poland. Shipping worldwide.
